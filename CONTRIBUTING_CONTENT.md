# Contributing Content to Movie Streamer

Thank you for helping grow the `Movie Streamer` catalog.

This app only accepts **legal, openly licensed, or public-domain video content**. TMDB remains metadata-only; submitted videos must point to a lawful streaming source such as Archive.org.

## ✅ Accepted licenses

Submissions must use one of the following:

- Public Domain
- `CC0`
- `CC BY`
- `CC BY-SA`

> If your content uses another license, include the exact terms and confirm redistribution/streaming is allowed.

## 1. Host your video on Archive.org

Before submitting, upload the movie or short film to [Archive.org](https://archive.org/):

1. Create or sign in to your Archive.org account.
2. Upload the video file and any poster or artwork you want to share.
3. Fill in the title, description, creator, year, and license fields.
4. Confirm the public playback URL works without private access.
5. Copy the final Archive.org item URL for your submission.

## 2. Prepare the required information

Please include all of the following in your submission:

- **Title**
- **Short description / synopsis**
- **Archive.org video URL**
- **Poster image URL** (if available)
- **License information**
- **Attribution / creator credit**
- Optional: release year, runtime, language, subtitles availability

## 3. Submit using the GitHub issue template

Open the **“Submit Your Movie / Short Film”** issue template and provide the details above.

The template is intended for:

- Original indie shorts you own the rights to
- Public-domain films
- Creative Commons licensed works with redistribution permission

## Content review guidelines

Submissions may be declined if they:

- lack a valid public license,
- rely on pirated or unauthorized sources,
- contain broken or private links,
- omit attribution for CC-licensed work,
- include misleading metadata.

## Helpful tips

- Use clear poster art and accurate descriptions.
- Prefer direct Archive.org item pages over temporary or redirect-heavy URLs.
- If subtitles are available, mention them in the issue.
- Keep all metadata family-safe and accurate for TV browsing.

Thanks for contributing responsibly.
